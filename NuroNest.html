How It Works

Simple. Powerful. Yours.

Five steps to transform how you study and work.

01

Create Your Profile

Sign up in seconds. Choose your goals — studying, fitness, creative work — and let NeuroNest build your personal focus blueprint using habit science principles from James Clear's Atomic Habits.

02

Connect Your Apps

Link Notion, Todoist, Google Calendar, and Spotify. NeuroNest pulls your tasks and syncs your schedule automatically — no duplicate data entry.

03

Log Focus Sessions

Start a timer, work in deep focus, and log every session. The more you log, the smarter your AI coach becomes at predicting your best hours.

04

Track Habits & Distractions

Mark daily habits complete and flag distractions. NeuroNest spots your patterns and helps you neutralize them — using techniques from Cognitive Behavioral Therapy.

05

Earn Rewards & Improve

Watch your focus score rise, collect badges, and receive AI-generated weekly reports showing exactly how far you've come.
